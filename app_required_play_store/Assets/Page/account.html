When you delete your account, your profile, photos, videos, comments, likes and followers will be permanently removed.


Information Collection and Use

By deleting your account, all data that has been uploaded or used within this application will be permanently lost. The account deletion process can only be carried out through the app and cannot be undone once completed. Please note that there is no feature to back up data. To delete your account, please follow the steps available in the settings menu of the app.
